Row Notes - Configuration

Pick the worksheet to annotate and the field that uniquely identifies each row. Then choose which of its columns to show, add your own text/dropdown columns, and put everything in the order you want.

Must be unique per row (e.g. Campaign ID). Notes are keyed to this value, so they survive re-filtering and re-sorting.

Source columns to show

Your columns

Add as many as you like — each is either free text or a dropdown with your own options.

Column order

Use the arrows to interleave your columns with the source columns however you like. Edit lets you rename any column's display label, including source columns.
