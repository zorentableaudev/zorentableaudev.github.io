Who I Am

About Me

I'm Zoren Cordon, a Tableau Developer and BI Consultant with 8 years of experience building data solutions that help teams make smarter, faster decisions.

I specialize in marketing campaign analytics, dashboard development, and data source design — primarily using Tableau Cloud and Google BigQuery. I've supported media buyers and analysts across Facebook, Google, and TikTok campaign reporting.

I'm passionate about turning messy data into clean, governed, and meaningful visuals. Whether you need a one-time dashboard build or an ongoing BI partner, I bring structure, standards, and storytelling to every engagement.
